    RULES
  • Players alternate turns dealing cards face up into the central pile
  • A player can "slap" at any time, with several outcomes!
  • If a jack is on top of the central pile when a player slaps, the central pile is added to their hand
  • Same rule applies to doubles, and sandwiches(a pair with any random card in the middle)
  • If a player slaps when none of those examples are true, that player forfeits their top card to the other's hand
  • If a player runs out of cards, they will be on their last stand where they have one chance to get back into the game
  • The player with cards remaining will deal until a jack is revealed and the empty player will be able to try and slap
  • Doubles and sandwiches are no longer valid for the player on their last stand
  • The empty player may only attempt to slap on a jack, if they are successful then they can reenter the game with the newly added central pile in their hand
  • If however the player who is out of cards slaps something other than a jack, or the other player slaps that jack first, the empty player loses
    CONTROLS
  • To start up a new game and split up the deck evenly press the start new game button at the top
  • Player 1: press q to deal a card into the middle, and press f to slap!
  • Player 2: press p to deal a card into the middle, and press j to slap!
